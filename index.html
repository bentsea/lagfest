Hall Cosplay

Hall Cosplay is a more relaxed form of cosplay competition based solely on craftsmanship, with no performance portion. Because of its flexible times and short duration for each competitor, it's a great option for those who have a busy convention schedule or don't want to go on stage.

LAG Fest is an annual anime, gaming, arts, and Japanese pop culture festival making its debut in 2016. The event will take place November 18-20 at the Ramada Lafayette Convention Center. Meet up with your friends or make some new ones at this year’s LAG Fest!

Sign up for a weekend of fun filled programming and meet your favorite voice actors, Cosplayers, and more. This show is looking to change the way people experience fandom with new programming and a new way for the community to come together.
